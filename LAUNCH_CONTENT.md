### Full Description
**DevToolkit** is a free, open-source suite of developer utilities that runs entirely in your browser.

**No accounts. No ads. No tracking.** Just fast, private tools that work offline.

## Features
🛠️ **JSON Formatter** - Format, validate & minify with syntax highlighting  
🔐 **Base64 Encoder** - Encode/decode with full Unicode support  
🔗 **URL Encoder** - Quick encode/decode for APIs and debugging  
📝 **Markdown Preview** - Live preview with GitHub-style rendering  
🎨 **Color Contrast** - WCAG 2.1 AA & AAA compliance checker

## Why DevToolkit?
Most online tools:
❌ Require accounts for basic tasks  
❌ Show ads and slow you down  
❌ Send your data to unknown servers  
❌ Don't work offline

DevToolkit is different:
✅ Open source (MIT license)  
✅ Zero tracking - everything stays local  
✅ Lightning fast - pure client-side  
✅ Works offline - perfect for travel  
✅ No build step - just HTML/CSS/JS

## Perfect For
• Quick data conversions during development  
• Debugging API responses  
• Writing documentation with live preview  
• Accessibility compliance checking  
• Privacy-conscious workflows  
• Airplane/coffee shop coding (works offline!)

## Tech Stack
Pure HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks, no dependencies, maximum performance.

## Introduction

As developers, we use online tools every day for quick tasks - formatting JSON, encoding Base64, checking color contrast. But most of these tools come with compromises: ads, forced signups, or data privacy concerns.

I built **DevToolkit** to solve this.

## What is DevToolkit?

A free, open-source suite of 5 essential developer utilities:

1. **JSON Formatter & Validator** - Format, minify, and validate with syntax highlighting
2. **Base64 Encoder/Decoder** - Full Unicode support for text encoding
3. **URL Encoder/Decoder** - Essential for API testing and debugging
4. **Markdown Preview** - Live preview with GitHub-style rendering
5. **Color Contrast Checker** - WCAG 2.1 AA & AAA compliance testing

## Key Features

✅ **No accounts required** - Open and use immediately  
✅ **No ads or tracking** - Privacy-first by design  
✅ **Works offline** - Perfect for flights and coffee shops  
✅ **Open source** - MIT licensed, contributions welcome  
✅ **Zero dependencies** - Pure HTML, CSS, vanilla JS

## Why This Matters

Unlike most online tools, DevToolkit:
- Never sends your data to servers
- Requires zero setup or accounts
- Loads instantly (no bloated frameworks)
- Works without internet after initial load

## Try It
